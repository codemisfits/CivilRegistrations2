Birth Registration

All births that occur within Botswana regardless of the parents’ nationality have to be registered in the Botswana Civil and National Registry.

Death Registration

All deaths that occur in Botswana regardless of nationality must be registered to keep the national registry up to date.

Change of Name

This service allows citizens of Botswana to change their official name, as registered in the Civil and National Registry.

Get Certificates

This service allows citizens of Botswana to now get digital brith and death certificates.

About Us

Ministry of Nationality, Immigration And Gender Affairs

The Ministry is a provider of essential services of civil registration and vital statistics, movement of persons across borders and gender equality which are important for the social and economic development of the country.

Departments/Sections of the Ministry

  • Department of Civil and National Registration
  • Department of Immigration and Citizenship
  • Department of Gender Affairs

Department of Civil and National Registration

The department’s responsibilities include registration of births, deaths, marriages, societies; issuance of Omang, and processing change of surname.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can i get a duplicate of a Birth Certficate?

    To obtain a duplicate of the birth certificate, someone with legal capacity and a valid national identity card for citizens or Passport for foreign nationals can collect his or her own birth certificate.

  • How much does Change Of Name Cost?

    The cost of obtaining this service is BWP100.00

  • What is needed for Death that occurred outside health institutions?

    1. Letter of confirmation of death from Kgosi or local authority.

    2.     Statement from declarant and certified copy of their valid national identity document.

    3.     Statements from two witnesses who know of the death and a certified copy of their valid national identity Document.
